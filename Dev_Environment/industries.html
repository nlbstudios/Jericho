Our Industries

Jericho House Services

Our industries department, known as JH Services, offers a number of services for the surrounding community, including landscaping, labor-ready jobs, remodeling, painting, and moving. Performing the duties of these jobs allows the men to sow back into the ministry of The Jericho House, while also easing the financial burden placed on their families. We strive to keep our program costs as low as possible, and these services help us achieve that goal. Furthermore, learning to deal with the demands of work is a vital part of the recovery process. Hiring JH Services is a great way to sow into the lives of the men and families on the road to recovery, while having your needs met in the process! If you are already paying for these services, why not use that money to bless these men and their families? If you live within 30 miles of Helen, Georgia, give us a call at 706-878-0015, and we will schedule a time to meet with you about your needs.
